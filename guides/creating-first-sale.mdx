This guide walks you through processing your first sale at the billing counter, from selecting items to collecting payment.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:
- Been assigned to a branch by your manager or owner
- At least one item with variants in your inventory
- Access to the POS (Point of Sale) interface

If you see a "No branch assigned" message, contact your manager to assign you to a branch before proceeding.

## Understanding the POS Dashboard

When you open the POS interface, you'll see:

- **Bills today**: Number of sales processed today and total amount billed
- **Collected today**: Total payments received today and pending amounts
- **Outstanding**: Total unpaid amounts across all sales
- **Active cart**: Where you'll build the current sale
- **Recent sales**: Latest 10 invoices issued at your branch

## Processing a Sale

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a New Sale">
    Click the **"Start new sale"** button in the top-right corner of the POS dashboard.
    
    This opens the active cart where you'll add items and customer details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Items to Cart">
    Search and select items from your inventory:
    
    1. Search by item name, code, or SKU
    2. Select the specific variant (size and color)
    3. Enter the quantity
    4. The system automatically calculates the line total based on the base price
    
    You can add multiple items to the same sale. Each item shows:
    - SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)
    - Size label
    - Color
    - Unit price
    - Quantity
    - Line total
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Customer (Optional)">
    You can process walk-in sales without customer details, but adding a customer helps with:
    
    - Tracking purchase history
    - Managing outstanding payments
    - Building customer relationships
    
    To add a customer:
    1. Search by name or phone number
    2. Select an existing customer or create a new one
    3. For new customers, enter their full name and phone number
    
    Walk-in customers are labeled as "Walk-in" in the sales list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply Discounts (Optional)">
    If you have permission, you can apply discounts:
    
    - Line-item discounts on individual products
    - Overall discount on the entire sale
    
    The cart automatically recalculates totals when you apply discounts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the Sale Summary">
    Before collecting payment, verify:
    
    - **Subtotal**: Sum of all items before discounts
    - **Discount total**: Total discounts applied
    - **Tax total**: Applicable taxes (if configured)
    - **Total amount**: Final amount to collect
  </Step>

  <Step title="Collect Payment">
    Click **"Collect payment"** to process the transaction:
    
    1. Enter the payment amount
    2. Select payment method:
       - Cash
       - UPI
       - Card
       - Other
    3. Add payment reference (optional, for UPI/card transactions)
    4. Add notes if needed
    
    The system calculates:
    - **Paid amount**: What the customer paid
    - **Due amount**: Any remaining balance
    
    You can accept partial payments - the system tracks outstanding dues automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the Sale">
    After collecting payment:
    
    1. The system generates a unique invoice number
    2. The sale appears in the "Recent sales" list
    3. The "Bills today" and "Collected today" stats update automatically
    4. The cart clears for the next transaction
    
    Your invoice number format is typically the sale number in uppercase (e.g., "ABC12345").
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Holding a Sale

If you need to pause a transaction (customer needs to get more items, take a call, etc.):

1. Click **"Hold sale"**
2. The system saves the cart
3. You can start a new sale for other customers
4. Resume the held sale later from the held sales list

## Understanding Sale Status

In the recent sales list, each sale shows:

- **Invoice number**: Unique identifier for the transaction
- **Customer**: Name or "Walk-in" for anonymous customers
- **Total**: Final amount of the sale
- **Status**: Either "Paid" with amount or "Due" with pending amount
- **Date**: When the sale was processed

<Accordion title="What if I make a mistake?">
  If you make an error:
  
  - **Before completing**: Simply remove items from the cart or cancel the sale
  - **After completing**: Contact your manager to process a return or exchange transaction
  
  Managers and owners have access to return and exchange workflows.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I edit sales after they're completed?">
  No, completed sales cannot be edited to maintain financial integrity. If corrections are needed:
  
  - Process a return for the original sale
  - Create a new sale with correct details
  
  All transactions maintain a complete audit trail.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I handle split payments?">
  Customers can pay using multiple methods:
  
  1. Accept the first payment (e.g., ₹5,000 cash)
  2. The system calculates remaining due amount
  3. Record additional payments against the same sale
  4. Each payment is tracked separately with its method and reference
  
  This is useful for large purchases where customers pay partly by card and partly by cash.
</Accordion>
